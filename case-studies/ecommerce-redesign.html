The Challenge

Problem

Farmers markets are community-first, but the online experience is often fragmented. We needed a single destination where people can find markets fast, trust vendors, and shop without confusion.

  • Discovery was scattered: users had to bounce between multiple sources to find market info and vendors.
  • Commerce wasn‚Äôt cohesive: browsing markets, vendors, and products didn‚Äôt feel connected as one journey.
  • Checkout expectations were unclear (account requirement, pickup vs delivery), creating drop-off risk.
  • The platform needed a scalable UI system so adding markets/vendors/products wouldn‚Äôt degrade consistency.
Objectives

Goals

  • Improve browse-to-cart conversion with clearer product cards, hierarchy, and CTAs.
  • Reduce checkout abandonment by making ordering steps and pickup/delivery choices explicit.
  • Ship a clean, repeatable UI foundation that supports fast iteration and expansion.
